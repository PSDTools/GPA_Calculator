GPA Calculator

How to use!

Input number of classes you are taking and press "Submit", then input your grades under "Grades:" and press "Calculate GPA and Save" to calculate your gpa. Putting in class names is optional.

Your class data will then be saved as a cookie to your device. This is so if you refresh, your data is saved!
